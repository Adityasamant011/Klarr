Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload data, or contact us. This includes your name, email address, and Shopify export data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide our bookkeeping service, process payments, send communications, and improve our service. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your financial data is processed through your own Google Sheet. We do not store your Shopify data on our servers. Account information is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) with industry-standard security.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session and track referral links. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing and Firebase for authentication. These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at klarr.space@gmail.com.
