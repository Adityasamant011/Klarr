FAQ

Common questions

What exactly do I get? +
Every month you get three financial statements: Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow. All transactions are categorized. Delivered as a clean Google Sheet within 24 hours of you sending us your Shopify export.
How do I send you my data? +
Export orders, payouts, and fees from Shopify. Send us the CSV files. Takes about 2 minutes. We handle everything from there.
Is my financial data safe? +
Yes. We never store your data on our servers. Everything is processed through your own Google Sheet. You own your data 100%.
Can my accountant use the output? +
Absolutely. The output is a standard Google Sheet with categorized transactions and tax summaries. Any CPA can work with it directly.
What if I'm not happy? +
Cancel anytime. If our work doesn't impress you, let us know — we'll make it right or refund you. No long-term contracts.
How is this different from QuickBooks? +
QuickBooks is DIY software — you still do the work. Klarr is a full service. We do everything for you. Your only job is to send us your Shopify export.
