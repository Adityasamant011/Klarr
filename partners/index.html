FAQ

How does tracking work?
When someone clicks your referral link, we track them with a cookie. If they sign up within 90 days, you get credit for the referral.
How much do I earn?
20% of the customer's monthly plan. Growth plan ($149/mo) = $29.80/mo commission. Pro plan ($249/mo) = $49.80/mo. Starter ($99/mo) = $19.80/mo.
When do I get paid?
Commissions accrue monthly. When your balance reaches $25, you can request a payout via PayPal or bank transfer.
Is there a contract?
No. You can leave anytime. No exclusivity requirements.
Can I see my referrals?
Yes. Your partner dashboard shows clicks, signups, active customers, and total earnings.
