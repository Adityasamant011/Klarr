FAQ

How does tracking work?
When someone clicks your referral link, we track them with a 90-day cookie. If they sign up within 90 days, you get credit.
How much do I earn?
20% of the customer's monthly plan. That's $19.80/mo per referral for as long as they stay.
When do I get paid?
Commissions accrue monthly. When your balance reaches $25, request a payout via PayPal or bank transfer. Payouts processed within 7 business days.
Is there a contract?
No. Leave anytime. No exclusivity requirements.
What if a referred customer cancels?
You only earn while they are active. If they cancel, you stop earning commission for that customer. No penalties.
